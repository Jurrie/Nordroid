Privacy Policy

Thank you for using Nordroid!

We value your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information.

1. No Personal Information Collected

We do not collect, store, or share any personal or sensitive information from users.

2. No Sensitive Permissions

Our app does not request or use any sensitive device permissions, such as access to your camera, microphone, location, contacts, or storage.

3. No Third-Party Tracking

We do not use third-party tools (like analytics or advertising services) that track your behavior in the app.

4. Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, we will make changes by updating this text.

5. In Case We Make a Boo-Boo

Do you think our app is not adhering to this privacy policy? That's a bug! Please contact us and let us know.

Nordroid has a GitHub where you can report issues. Nordroid is not created by Clavia; please do not bug them with questions.
